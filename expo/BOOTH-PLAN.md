## Demo Script (2-minute pitch)

### Opening (10 sec)
"Hey! Do you have a Google listing for your business?"

### Problem (20 sec)
"Most local businesses set up their Google listing once and never touch it again. Meanwhile, the businesses that post weekly, respond to reviews, and have local pages — they're the ones getting the calls."

### Solution (30 sec)
"LocalBeacon does all of that automatically. We post to your Google listing every week, create local pages for every city you serve, and draft replies to your reviews. You just approve what we write — or let it run hands-free."

### Proof (20 sec)
[Show the dashboard on laptop]
"Here's what it looks like — these are this week's posts, ready to go. And these are city pages we built automatically. Takes about 2 minutes to set up."

### Close (20 sec)
"You can try it free — just scan the QR code or go to localbeacon.ai. No credit card needed. If you want the full autopilot, it's $29 a month. Way cheaper than hiring someone."

### If they manage multiple businesses (agency pitch)
"Oh, you manage clients? We have an Agency plan for $79/mo — multi-client dashboard, white-label reports with your branding. Your clients never see us."
