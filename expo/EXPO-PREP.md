## 60-Second Demo Script (for Bob)

> *[Person walks up to booth]*
>
> **David:** "Hey! Quick question — when someone asks Google or ChatGPT for a plumber near them, does your business show up?"
>
> *[Usually: "I don't know" or "Probably not"]*
>
> **David:** "Most local businesses don't — and it's costing them calls. Let me show you something. What's your website?"
>
> *[Type their URL into localbeacon.ai/check]*
>
> **David:** "This runs a quick check on 15 things that affect whether Google and AI assistants recommend your business. Takes about 30 seconds."
>
> *[Show results]*
>
> **David:** "See that? You're at [score]/100. That means [specific recommendation]. The good news is most of these fixes are straightforward — that's exactly what LocalBeacon does. We write your Google posts, build local pages for every city you serve, and make sure AI search engines can find you."
>
> *[Hand them a one-pager]*
>
> **David:** "Scan that QR code and you can run the check yourself anytime — it's free. And if you want us to handle the rest, plans start at $49 a month."

**Key rules:**
- Never say "AI" when describing what LocalBeacon does — say "we handle it"
- Always start with THEIR problem ("does your business show up?")
- Always demo with THEIR actual website
- Don't oversell — the score speaks for itself
- End with the one-pager + QR code, not a hard close
