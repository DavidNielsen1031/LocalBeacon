## Target List Criteria (MN-based agencies)

**Ideal target:**
- Web design or digital marketing agency
- Based in Minnesota (start local, expand later)
- <20 employees
- Manages websites for local service businesses (plumbers, dentists, HVAC, etc.)
- Active online presence (has their own website, LinkedIn, Google listing)

**Where to find them:**
- Google: "web design agency [city] MN"
- LinkedIn: search for "web designer" in Minnesota
- Clutch.co: filter by location + service + company size
- Google Maps: "web design" in Minneapolis, St. Paul, Burnsville, etc.

**Goal:** 20 agencies outreached → 3-5 trial signups → 1 paid conversion
