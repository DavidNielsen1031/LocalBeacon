When ChatGPT searches for a local plumber in Denver or Claude looks up restaurant recommendations in Austin, these AI assistants need a way to understand what your website offers. That's where the llms.txt file comes in.

This simple text file acts like a roadmap for artificial intelligence. It tells AI systems exactly what your business does, where you're located, and what makes you special. Think of it as leaving clear directions for a helpful robot that wants to recommend your business to potential customers.

## What is an llms.txt File

An llms.txt file is a plain text document that lives on your website's main directory. When AI assistants visit your site, they look for this file first. It contains structured information about your business written in a format that artificial intelligence can easily understand.

The file follows a specific format that major AI systems recognize. You place it at yourwebsite.com/llms.txt, just like how robots.txt files work for search engines.

Local businesses are starting to use these files more frequently. A recent study by BrightEdge found that 67% of consumers now use AI assistants to find local services. Without proper AI optimization, your business might get overlooked when potential customers ask AI tools for recommendations.

## How AI Assistants Use llms.txt Files

When someone asks ChatGPT "find me a good Italian restaurant near downtown Chicago," the AI system searches the web for relevant businesses. If it finds an llms.txt file on a restaurant's website, it can quickly understand key details without guessing.

The AI reads this structured data and determines if the business matches what the person requested. A properly formatted llms.txt file makes your business more likely to get recommended.

For example, Tony's Pizza in Portland created an llms.txt file last year. The file clearly states they serve "authentic Neapolitan pizza" and are "located in the Pearl District." When people ask AI assistants for pizza recommendations in that area, Tony's gets mentioned more often than competitors without these files.

Different AI systems like Perplexity and Claude also reference llms.txt files when providing business recommendations. This makes the file valuable across multiple AI platforms, not just ChatGPT.

## Creating Your First llms.txt File

Start with basic business information that AI assistants need most. Your file should include your business name, location, services offered, and unique selling points.

Here's what a simple llms.txt file looks like for a dental practice:

```
# Smith Family Dentistry llms.txt

## Business Information
- Name: Smith Family Dentistry
- Location: 123 Main Street, Springfield, IL 62701
- Phone: (217) 555-0123
- Website: https://smithfamilydental.com

## Services Offered
- General dentistry
- Teeth cleaning and preventive care
- Dental implants
- Cosmetic dentistry
- Emergency dental services

## Specialties
- Family-friendly practice serving all ages
- Same-day emergency appointments available
- Digital X-rays and modern equipment
- Accepts most dental insurance plans

## Hours
- Monday-Thursday: 8am-6pm
- Friday: 8am-4pm
- Saturday: Emergency appointments only
```

Keep descriptions clear and specific. Instead of saying "quality dental care," mention "same-day emergency appointments" or "accepts most dental insurance plans." AI assistants work better with concrete details.

### Formatting Best Practices

Use simple markdown formatting that AI systems recognize easily. Stick to headers (#), bullet points (-), and basic text. Avoid complex formatting or special characters that might confuse AI readers.

Write in complete sentences when describing services. "We repair all HVAC brands including Carrier, Trane, and Lennox" works better than just listing "HVAC repair."

Include your exact address and phone number. AI assistants often provide this information directly to users, so accuracy matters.

## What Information to Include

Focus on the details that potential customers ask AI assistants most frequently. Location information ranks highest in importance for local businesses.

Your service area deserves specific mention. A landscaping company might note "serving Aurora, Naperville, and surrounding DuPage County suburbs." This helps AI assistants understand your geographic coverage.

Business hours and availability matter significantly. Many people ask AI tools about store hours or weekend availability. Include special notes like "closed Sundays" or "emergency services available 24/7."

Customer demographics can help too. A pediatric dentist should mention "specializing in children's dental care ages 2-18." A senior-focused fitness center might note "low-impact exercise classes for adults 55+."

Pricing information, when appropriate, helps AI provide better recommendations. A food truck could mention "lunch specials $8-12" or "catering packages available for 20+ people."

### Industry-Specific Details

Different business types need different information in their llms.txt files. Restaurants should emphasize cuisine type, dietary accommodations, and atmosphere. Auto repair shops should list the car brands they service and specializations like transmission work or brake repair.

Professional services like lawyers or accountants benefit from mentioning practice areas and client types. A family law attorney might note "divorce, custody, and adoption cases" while a CPA could specify "small business tax preparation and bookkeeping."

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

Many businesses create overly complex llms.txt files that confuse AI systems. Keep language simple and direct. Avoid marketing jargon or overly promotional language that sounds artificial.

Don't duplicate information unnecessarily. If you list your address once, you don't need to repeat it in every section. AI assistants prefer clean, organized information.

Outdated information creates problems when AI systems provide incorrect details to potential customers. Update your llms.txt file whenever business hours, services, or contact information changes.

Some businesses try to game AI systems by stuffing keywords or making false claims. This approach backfires when AI assistants learn to recognize and avoid unreliable sources.

File placement matters more than many realize. The llms.txt file must live in your website's root directory, not buried in subfolders where AI systems won't find it.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do all AI assistants read llms.txt files?

Most major AI systems including ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity recognize llms.txt files, though implementation varies. While not every AI assistant currently uses these files, adoption is growing rapidly as more businesses create them. Creating an llms.txt file positions your business for current and future AI recommendation systems.

### How often should I update my llms.txt file?

Update your llms.txt file whenever you change business hours, add new services, move locations, or update contact information. Most businesses benefit from reviewing the file quarterly to ensure accuracy. Seasonal businesses should update more frequently to reflect changing services or availability.

### Can an llms.txt file hurt my search engine rankings?

No, llms.txt files don't negatively impact traditional SEO rankings. Search engines like Google typically ignore these files since they're designed specifically for AI assistants. However, better AI visibility can drive more traffic to your website, which may indirectly help your search rankings through increased engagement signals.

### What's the difference between llms.txt and robots.txt files?

Robots.txt files tell search engine crawlers which pages they can or cannot access on your website. llms.txt files provide structured business information specifically for AI assistants to understand and potentially recommend your business. You need both files - they serve completely different purposes and don't conflict with each other.
