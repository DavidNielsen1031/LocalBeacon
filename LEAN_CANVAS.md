# LocalBeacon.ai — Lean Canvas

*Drafted: March 3, 2026 | SVPG Product Masterclass exercise*

---

## Problem (Top 3)
1. **Local businesses are invisible to AI search.** When someone asks ChatGPT "find me a plumber in Burnsville," most SMBs don't show up — they have zero AI-readable presence (no structured data, no llms.txt, no FAQ schema).
2. **Web designers/agencies can't keep up with AI discovery.** Their clients expect to be found, but AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is a brand-new discipline — no tools exist to help agencies deliver it at scale.
3. **SMB owners don't understand the shift.** They're still thinking "Google SEO" while 800M+ weekly users are asking ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity for local recommendations. They're losing leads they don't even know exist.

## Solution (Top 3 Features)
1. **AI Readiness Checker** — 15-point website scan, 0-100 score, plain-English fix list. Bob the plumber can understand it.
2. **One-click AI Discovery Files** — Generate llms.txt, FAQ schema, LocalBusiness schema, and AEO-optimized content from a simple business profile. Download and hand to your "website guy."
3. **Agency Dashboard** — Manage multiple client businesses, generate content at scale, track AI readiness scores over time.

## Unique Value Proposition
**"When someone asks AI to find a business like yours, make sure you're the answer."**

Nobody else does AEO for local businesses. Google SEO tools are everywhere. AI discovery tools for SMBs? We're first.
