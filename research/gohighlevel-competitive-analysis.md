# GoHighLevel Competitive Analysis
*Created: 2026-03-18*

## What GoHighLevel Is
- All-in-one CRM + marketing platform ($97-497/mo, real cost $400-600/mo with add-ons)
- Funnels, email/SMS marketing, pipeline management, booking, website builder, reviews, AI chatbot
- Agency-focused: white-label, resell to clients, unlimited sub-accounts on higher tiers
- 1M+ businesses use it
- Hidden costs: SMS ($0.0079/segment), calls ($0.014/min), email ($0.675/1K), AI ($20-100+/mo extra)

## Pricing Tiers
- **Starter:** $97/mo — 1 user, 3 sub-accounts, basic CRM/pipeline/automation
- **Unlimited:** $297/mo — unlimited users/sub-accounts, priority support, rebilling
- **SaaS Pro:** $497/mo — white-label reselling, advanced API, AI conversation tools

## What GHL Does Well
- Review management (request + respond)
- SMS/email marketing automation
- Appointment booking
- Website/funnel builder
- Recently added: GBP optimization (edit profile from dashboard), basic SEO tools
- Sub-account system for agencies managing multiple clients

## What GHL Does NOT Do (Our Advantage)
- ❌ AI Readiness / AEO scoring — no concept of this
- ❌ llms.txt generation
- ❌ AI-optimized content specifically for ChatGPT/Perplexity/Claude discovery
- ❌ Service area page generation for local SEO
- ❌ Schema markup generation
- ❌ Blog content engine tuned for AI answer engines
- ❌ Competitor AI visibility comparison

## Differentiation Pitch
> "GoHighLevel is a CRM. It helps you manage leads you already have. LocalBeacon gets you discovered by the leads you're missing — the 40% who now ask ChatGPT instead of Googling. We're not replacing GoHighLevel. We're the thing that feeds it."

## Key Framing
- GHL = **manage** customers. LocalBeacon = **get found** by customers.
- GHL optimizes for Google Search (old). LocalBeacon optimizes for AI Search (new).
- GHL is $400-600/mo all-in. LocalBeacon is $49-99/mo. They stack, not compete.
- For salon owners using GHL: "Keep GoHighLevel for your CRM and booking. Add LocalBeacon so ChatGPT actually recommends your salon when people ask."
