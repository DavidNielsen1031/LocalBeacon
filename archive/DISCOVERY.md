# DISCOVERY.md — LocalBeacon.ai

## Customer Discovery Program (Steve Blank Method)

> "No facts exist inside the building, only opinions." — Steve Blank

**Goal:** Recruit 6 early evangelists from the B2B2B channel (web designers/agencies managing SMB clients). Find a single solution that works for all 6. Get them to commit as references.

### Target Segment
**Web designers and small agencies** who manage 5-50 local business clients (plumbers, HVAC, dentists, roofers, lawyers). They already handle SEO, social, and websites — but local visibility is manual, fragmented, and underserved by their current tools.

### Why This Segment First
- They have the problem at scale (multiplied across all their clients)
- They're technically capable enough to use a dashboard tool
- They can become force-multipliers (one agency = 20+ SMB end-users)
- They match our Agency tier ($99/mo) — highest revenue per customer

### Evangelist Recruitment Plan

**Channel 1: Ramsey EDA Business Expo (Apr 25)**
- Register by Apr 10 deadline
- Goal: Meet 3-5 web designers/agency owners in person
- Bring: Laptop with live demo, not slides
- Ask: "How do you currently handle local visibility for your clients? What's the most painful part?"

**Channel 2: Online Outreach (Immediate)**
- LinkedIn: Search "web designer" + "local business" in MN area
- Facebook Groups: Local business marketing groups, freelance web designer communities
- Reddit: r/webdesign, r/SEO, r/smallbusiness — lurk for pain signals, DM people describing the problem
- Goal: 3-5 conversations in the next 2 weeks

**Channel 3: Existing Network**
- David's professional network — anyone who runs a small agency or does freelance web work?
- Turnberry colleagues who know marketing agency contacts?

### Interview Script (First Contact)

1. "Tell me about the local businesses you manage. How many? What industries?"
2. "How do you currently help them get found locally? Google Business Profile? Social? Directories?"
3. "What's the most time-consuming part of managing their local presence?"
4. "Have you tried any tools for this? What worked, what didn't?"
5. "If I could show you something that handles [their pain point] — would you give me 30 minutes next week to walk through it?"

**What we're listening for:**
- Janky workarounds (spreadsheets, manual posting, copy-pasting across platforms)
- Pain frequency (daily annoyance vs. quarterly headache)
- Willingness to switch from current approach
- Whether AEO (AI discoverability) resonates or confuses them

### Evangelist Commitment Ask

Once we've found someone with genuine pain:
- "We're building this specifically for agencies like yours. I'd like to work closely with you for the next 6-8 weeks — show you prototypes, get your feedback, iterate together."
- "In exchange, if we build something that actually solves this for you, would you be willing to be a reference customer we can point other agencies to?"
- Skin in the game signal: Will they commit time? (Weekly 30-min sessions)
