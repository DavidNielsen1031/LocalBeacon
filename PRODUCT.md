# LocalBeacon

## What It Is
LocalBeacon helps local businesses get found by AI search engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude — not just Google. It delivers AI Engine Optimization (AEO): industry-specific landing pages, automated blog content, AEO audit scoring, and llms.txt/schema generation. 60+ industry verticals at launch. Primary go-to-market is B2B2B: web designers and digital agencies who manage 10-50+ SMB clients.

## Who It's For
- **Primary:** Web designers and small digital agencies managing local business clients (plumbers, HVAC, dentists, roofers, lawyers). They handle SEO, social, and websites — local AI visibility is a painful new gap.
- **Secondary:** Tech-savvy SMB owners who manage their own web presence and understand the shift from Google to AI search.

## Positioning
- **One-liner:** When someone asks AI to find a business like yours, make sure you're the answer.
- **Problem:** AI assistants are replacing Google for local searches. SMBs have zero presence in AI results. Traditional SEO doesn't help with AEO (Answer Engine Optimization).
- **Solution:** Done-for-you AEO — AI Readiness Checker (0-100 score), one-click AI discovery files (llms.txt, FAQ schema, LocalBusiness schema), agency dashboard for multi-client management.
- **Differentiator:** First platform focused specifically on AEO for local businesses. 11 tools already built and live. First-mover — no direct AEO competitor in the local SMB space.

## Pricing
- Free: AEO audit + basic recommendations (no signup required)
- Pro: $49/mo — single business, all tools, managed content
- Agency: $99/mo — multi-client dashboard, bulk content generation

## Competitive Landscape
No direct AEO competitor for local businesses exists. Traditional SEO tools (Moz, SEMrush, BrightLocal) don't address AI discoverability. We're first. Risk: this space will attract competitors once AEO becomes mainstream.

## Vision
Validate demand through 6 evangelist agencies (Steve Blank method). If validated: build white-label agency tier, GBP posting automation, and recurring AI content for clients. Path to $5K MRR via 50 agencies at $99/mo.
